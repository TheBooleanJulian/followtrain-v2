🔒 PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

FollowTrain ("FollowTrain," "we," "our," or "us") is designed for temporary, frictionless social networking. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is automatically deleted.

By using FollowTrain, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described below.


1. THE 72-HOUR DATA RETENTION RULE

FollowTrain is intentionally built as a transient service.

All train-related data — including but not limited to:

  • Display names
  • Social media URLs
  • Biographical text
  • Participant lists
  • Train metadata

— is automatically and permanently deleted from our production database no later than 72 hours after train creation, unless deleted sooner by the Train Host.

We do not archive expired trains. We do not maintain permanent public profiles.

Important Exception

Temporary server logs, security logs, and automated backups may retain limited technical metadata (e.g., IP address, timestamp, request logs) for a short operational period solely for:

  • Abuse prevention
  • Security monitoring
  • System debugging
  • Legal compliance

Such logs are not used for marketing purposes.


2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

FollowTrain does not require user accounts, passwords, or identity verification.

We collect only the information you voluntarily submit when you:

  • Create a train
  • Join a train

This may include:

  • Display name
  • Social media URLs
  • Short biography text

We also automatically collect limited technical data, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Basic analytics data
  • Timestamp of access

This data is used strictly for functionality, security, and service improvement.


3. PUBLIC VISIBILITY WARNING

FollowTrain is a public-sharing tool.

Any information you submit to a train is visible to anyone who possesses the corresponding:

  • Train URL
  • QR Code

We do not provide private trains or access controls beyond possession of the link.

You should not submit:

  • Government ID numbers
  • Home addresses
  • Personal phone numbers
  • Financial information
  • Sensitive personal data
  • Information you would not post publicly

You are solely responsible for the information you choose to disclose.


4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES & AVATARS

FollowTrain does not scrape social media platforms.

To generate visual avatars, we may use third-party services (such as ui-avatars.com) that create stylized profile images based solely on the display name you provide.

These services do not receive your social login credentials or access your social accounts.

FollowTrain may also use:

  • Basic infrastructure hosting providers
  • Analytics providers
  • Content delivery networks (CDNs)

These providers process limited technical data as necessary to operate the service.


5. COOKIES & LOCAL STORAGE

We do not use invasive advertising trackers.

We may use:

  • Basic session cookies
  • Browser Local Storage

For purposes such as:

  • Saving UI preferences (e.g., Dark Mode)
  • Maintaining temporary train state
  • Basic performance analytics

You may disable cookies in your browser, though some features may not function properly.


6. DATA SECURITY

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

However, no online platform can guarantee absolute security. Use of the service is at your own risk.


7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

FollowTrain is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child has submitted personal information, please contact us for removal.


8. DATA REMOVAL REQUESTS

Because all trains auto-delete within 72 hours, most data is short-lived.

If you require early deletion prior to automatic purge, you may contact the Train Host.


9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
